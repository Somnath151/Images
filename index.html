Overview

Radhe Developers ("we", "our", or "us") built the Photo Collage Maker application as a free app. This page informs users regarding our policies with respect to the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone who chooses to use our app.

By using Photo Collage Maker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal data on our own servers. However, certain third-party services integrated into the app may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

The categories of data that may be collected through third-party SDKs include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / GAID / IDFA)

Google AdMob (Advertising)

Photo Collage Maker uses Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised advertisements.

  • AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve interest-based ads
  • AdMob collects data such as device info, IP address, and ad interaction events
  • Ads may be targeted based on your interests, demographics, or prior interactions
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising via your device settings
🔗 To learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit policies.google.com/privacy. To opt out of interest-based advertising, visit adssettings.google.com.

On Android devices, you can reset or opt out of the Advertising ID by going to Settings → Google → Ads.

📊

Google Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with Photo Collage Maker. This helps us improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance the user experience.

  • Firebase logs app events (e.g. screen views, button taps, feature usage)
  • Session data including duration and frequency of use
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Crash logs and ANR (App Not Responding) reports via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Aggregate retention and engagement metrics
🔗 Firebase Analytics data is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy. All analytics data is anonymised and aggregated before analysis.

You can disable Firebase Analytics data collection by navigating to Settings → Google → Usage & diagnostics on your Android device.

🖼️

Photos & Camera Access

Photo Collage Maker requests access to your device's photo gallery to allow you to select images for your collage. This permission is used solely for the following purpose:

  • Reading images from your gallery when you tap a collage slot to add a photo
  • Saving the finished collage image to your gallery (Pictures/PhotoCollage folder)
🔒 We never upload your photos to any server. All photo processing (cropping, resizing, compositing) happens entirely on your device. Your images are never transmitted, stored, or shared with Radhe Developers or any third party.

The app uses the standard Android READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission (Android 13+) or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below). You can revoke this permission at any time from your device's Settings → Apps → Photo Collage Maker → Permissions.

💾

Storage & Saved Files

When you export a collage, the app writes the resulting JPEG image to your device's local storage under Pictures/PhotoCollage/. This file is stored on your device only and is accessible in your photo gallery app.

  • Files are saved locally — no cloud upload or sync occurs
  • We use Android's MediaStore API (Android 10+) for compliant gallery saving
  • No metadata or EXIF location data is embedded in exported collages
  • Deleting the app does not automatically remove previously saved collage images
