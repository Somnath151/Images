Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Sms Shield developed and published by RadheDevelopers. The application is provided as a service and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

Sms Shield may collect limited information automatically when you use the application. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage information
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

This information is used solely to improve application performance and user experience.

SMS Permissions and Message Processing

Sms Shield may request permission to receive, read, and send SMS messages in order to provide its core functionality.

If you choose to set Sms Shield as your device’s default SMS application, the app may process incoming and outgoing messages to provide spam filtering, message blocking, and message management features.

All SMS message processing is performed locally on your device. Sms Shield does not upload, store, or transmit your SMS content to external servers unless explicitly stated within the application.

Blocked messages are stored locally on your device so that you can review or delete them at any time.

You may enable or disable spam protection features within the app settings at any time.

Phone Permissions

If granted, phone-related permissions may be used to identify incoming message context or provide spam-related features. This information is processed locally and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Sms Shield may use trusted third-party services for analytics, advertising, or performance monitoring. These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics or Crash Reporting

These third-party providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Sms Shield does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

Information may be disclosed only if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

Data processed by Sms Shield is retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality. Locally stored blocked messages remain on your device until you delete them.

Security

We value your trust and implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Sms Shield does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all information collection by uninstalling the application using standard uninstall procedures available on your device.
